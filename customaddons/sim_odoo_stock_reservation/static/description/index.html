Stock Reservation in Odoo

Sales Stock Reservation

Product Reservation - Reserve Product in Odoo

Main Features:

  • Allow users to create a reservation from Sales Quotation.
  • Sales Stock reservation.
  • Product reservation feature on Sales order.
  • Allow users to unreserve stock once the customer confirms the order.
  • Email notification to Stock users during reservation from Sales Quote.
  • Allow users to choose which product(s) from the Sales Quotation line users want to reserve.
  • Allow users to choose how much quantity of product from Quotation they want to reserve.
  • Stock reservation records and pivot table for reservations.
  • Allow you to see the complete history of stock reservation on sales order.
  • Reservation Stock Location data created by our App which will allow the system to reserve stock/product under that location.
  • Unique sequence number of reservations.
  • For more details please check below screenshots and watch the video.
